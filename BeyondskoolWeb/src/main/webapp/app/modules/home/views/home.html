Our Mission

Most people try to learn critical life skills only after they graduate. Alas, it is too late. Unfortunately, schools don't teach this. The cost of education has gone up but the quality has remained the same. We firmly believe that these life skills should be learnt during the formative years of childhood to build a solid foundation.
BeyondSkool is on a mission to do just that - prepare children for tomorrow!
